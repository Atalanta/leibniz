Welcome to Leibniz

Leibniz is a library of classes, methods, matchers and expectations to make it easier to write acceptance tests for infrastructure and configuration management.

Although principally used in the context of Cucumber and Chef, the vision for Leibniz is to be the standard library used by anyone wanting to perform acceptance testing against infrastructure.

Leibniz is named after the 17th century mathematician, commonly agreed to be one of the founding fathers of integral calculus.
